# 03 — Business Model

## Revenue Philosophy

The South County Signal is a free newsletter that monetizes through advertising and sponsored content sold to local businesses. The model works because the audience is hyper-local, highly engaged, and exactly the customer base that South County restaurants, bars, venues, and retailers are trying to reach. Every ad is useful — that's the deal we make with our readers.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Sponsored Section Placements

Each newsletter section can be sponsored. The sponsor gets a "Presented by [Business]" tag and their business is woven into the content.

| Placement | Description | Rate (Launch) | Rate (5K subs) | Rate (10K subs) |
|-----------|-------------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **This Week's Move** | Headliner sponsor — top billing | $75/wk | $150/wk | $250/wk |
| **Featured Lineup Slot** | One sponsored event in the roundup | $40/wk | $75/wk | $125/wk |
| **Food & Drink Spotlight** | Full profile of a restaurant/bar | $50/wk | $100/wk | $200/wk |
| **Insider Deal** | Featured deal with code/offer | $25/wk | $50/wk | $100/wk |

### 2. Display Advertising

Traditional ad placements within the email layout.

| Placement | Description | Rate (Launch) | Rate (5K subs) | Rate (10K subs) |
|-----------|-------------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **Mid-Newsletter Ad** | Centered card between Lineup and Spotlight | $50/wk | $150/wk | $250/wk |
| **Footer Sponsor** | Logo + one-liner at bottom of every issue | $30/wk | $75/wk | $100/wk |

### 3. Seasonal Guides (Sponsored)

Deep-dive content pieces published 4–6 times per year, with sponsored category sections.

| Guide | Timing | Sponsor Slots | Rate per Slot |
|-------|--------|:---:|:---:|
| Summer Kickoff Guide | Late May | 6–8 | $200–500 |
| 4th of July Guide | Late June | 4–6 | $150–300 |
| Fall Foliage & Harvest | September | 6–8 | $200–500 |
| Holiday Gift & Markets | November | 8–10 | $200–500 |
| Best of South County (Annual) | January | 10–15 | $300–1,000 |

### 4. Affiliate & Partnership Revenue

- Eventbrite/ticket platform affiliate links (5–10% commission)
- Local business partnerships with rev-share on redeemed deals
- Beehiiv Ad Network revenue (available on Scale plan and above)

### 5. Premium Content (Future — Year 2+)

- Paid subscriber tier with exclusive deals, early access to seasonal guides
- "Signal Insider" membership with quarterly local business discounts bundle
- Sponsored email courses (e.g., "5-Day South County Food Tour" presented by a restaurant group)

## Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly fixed costs at launch:** ~$85
**Average revenue per ad placement:** ~$75 (blended across all placement types at launch pricing)
**Break-even:** 2 placements sold per month (achievable by month 7–8)

**Monthly fixed costs at scale:** ~$200
**Average revenue per ad placement at 10K subs:** ~$175
**Break-even at scale:** 2 placements sold per month (easily exceeded with 4–6 recurring advertisers)

## Scenario Analysis

### Conservative (Slow Growth)
- 12-month subscribers: 3,000
- 12-month revenue: $2,400
- Assumption: Minimal social media effort, organic only

### Moderate (Base Case)
- 12-month subscribers: 5,000
- 12-month revenue: $4,700
- Assumption: Consistent posting, 5–10 launch partners, light paid growth

### Aggressive (High Growth)
- 12-month subscribers: 8,000
- 12-month revenue: $9,600
- Assumption: Viral social content, strong partnerships, paid ads at month 4+, seasonal guides
