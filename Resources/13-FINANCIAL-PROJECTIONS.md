## Tax Considerations

This income should be reported as self-employment income (Schedule C). Since you're already navigating sole proprietor tax status with your trading activity, the newsletter fits naturally into the same structure. Key considerations:

- All tool costs, event attendance, dining (for content), and equipment are deductible business expenses
- Home office deduction applies if you have dedicated workspace
- Self-employment tax applies (15.3% on net earnings)
- Quarterly estimated tax payments will be needed once revenue exceeds ~$1,000/quarter
- Consider folding this into your LLC if you're transferring other business activities there already

See `14-LEGAL-CONSIDERATIONS.md` for more on entity structure.
