# 10 — Launch Plan

## 30-Day Pre-Launch Checklist

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Register domain: southcountysignal.com
- [ ] Set up Google Workspace (hello@southcountysignal.com)
- [ ] Create Beehiiv account, configure publication
- [ ] Connect custom domain, set up DNS/email auth (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- [ ] Design email template (or upload the HTML template from assets/)
- [ ] Create Instagram account (@southcountysignal), write bio, upload profile pic
- [ ] Create Facebook Page

### Week 2: Content Buffer
- [ ] Write 3 newsletter issues in advance (issues #1, #2, #3)
- [ ] Create the welcome email sequence (3 emails — see 06-TECH-STACK.md)
- [ ] Post 10 pieces of content to Instagram before announcing the newsletter
- [ ] Create a simple landing page on Beehiiv with email signup
- [ ] Design sign-up cards for physical distribution (Canva)

### Week 3: Partnerships
- [ ] Identify 10 local businesses for launch partnerships
- [ ] Send cold outreach emails (see 09-OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md, Template 3)
- [ ] Follow up with interested partners
- [ ] Confirm 5–10 businesses willing to cross-promote
- [ ] Deliver sign-up cards to partner locations

### Week 4: Launch Build-Up
- [ ] Announce launch date on Instagram (2 weeks out)
- [ ] Share "coming soon" content: behind-the-scenes, sneak peeks, local photos
- [ ] Post in local Facebook groups about the upcoming launch
- [ ] Ask friends, family, and personal network to subscribe
- [ ] Send personal emails/texts to 50–100 people asking them to subscribe and share
- [ ] Do a "subscriber count" countdown on Instagram Stories
- [ ] Create a one-page media kit (see 08-MEDIA-KIT.md)

## Launch Week Playbook

### Monday (4 days before)
- Final review of Issue #1
- Test send to yourself and 2–3 friends for feedback
- Post Instagram Reel: "This Thursday, something new for South County"

### Tuesday (3 days before)
- Fix any issues from test feedback
- Post Instagram carousel: "What to expect from The South County Signal"
- Share in Facebook groups: "Launching a free weekly events newsletter for South County — link in bio"

### Wednesday (1 day before)
- Final proofread, check all links
- Schedule send for Thursday 8:30 AM
- Prepare Thursday Instagram post + Story sequence

### Thursday — LAUNCH DAY
- 8:30 AM: Issue #1 sends
- 9:00 AM: Post to Instagram: "Issue #1 is live. Link in bio."
- 9:30 AM: Post in Facebook groups with a genuine, helpful message (not spam)
- 10:00 AM: Text your personal network: "I launched the thing — would love if you checked it out"
- Throughout day: Respond to every reply, DM, and comment
- 5:00 PM: Instagram Story with early stats ("300 people opened it today — let's get to 500")

### Friday (Day after)
- Check analytics: open rate, click rate, subscriber count
- Post Instagram Story: "This weekend in South County" quick hits from the newsletter
- Send thank-you messages to launch partners
