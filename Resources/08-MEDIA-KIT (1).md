# The South County Signal — Media Kit

## Advertise to the people who actually go out in South County.

The South County Signal is a free weekly email newsletter delivering curated events, dining, nightlife, and things to do in Southern Rhode Island to an engaged local audience of millennials and active adults.

---

## Our Audience

**Who reads the Signal:**
- 70% aged 28–55
- 85% live in or regularly visit South County (Narragansett, Wakefield, Westerly, Charlestown, North Kingstown, East Greenwich)
- 15% from Providence, Newport, and Connecticut (weekend visitors)
- High household income, experience-driven lifestyle
- Active social lives — they dine out, attend events, and support local businesses

**Engagement Metrics:**
- **Open rate:** 42–48% (industry average for newsletters: 21%)
- **Click-through rate:** 6–9% (industry average: 2.6%)
- **Read time:** 3–4 minutes per issue
- **Reply rate:** Readers actively reply to our emails (strong community engagement)

*Our audience doesn't just receive the newsletter — they read it, click through, and act on it.*

- **Rate: $100–200/week**

### Insider Deal
Feature an exclusive offer or discount for our readers in a high-visibility dark card with a redemption code.

### Footer Sponsor
Persistent "Presented By" placement at the bottom of every issue.
- Low-key, consistent brand exposure
- Ideal for brand awareness over time
- **Rate: $75–100/week** (4-week minimum)

## Why the Signal Works

**We're not social media.** Your post on Instagram competes with 500 other posts in a feed. In the Signal, your placement sits in a curated email that people chose to open and read for 3–4 minutes.

**We're not the newspaper.** Our audience is younger, more engaged, and actively looking for things to do this week — not catching up on yesterday's news.

**We're not a coupon mailer.** Every piece of content in the Signal is written with personality and editorial judgment. Your ad doesn't look like an ad — it looks like a recommendation from a friend.

**Our readers act.** With open rates 2x the industry average and click rates 3x the industry average, Signal readers don't just see your business — they visit it.
