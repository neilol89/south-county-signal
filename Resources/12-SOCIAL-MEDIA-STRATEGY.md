# 12 — Social Media Strategy

## Platform Priority

1. **Instagram** — Primary growth and engagement channel
2. **Facebook** — Groups for launch seeding, Page for broader reach
3. **TikTok** — Future channel for viral local content (Phase 2)

## Instagram Strategy (@southcountysignal)

### Bio
```
Your weekly cheat sheet for Southern RI 🌊
Events • Food • Music • Nightlife • Beach Life
Every Thursday in your inbox 👇
[Linktree or direct Beehiiv signup link]
```

### Content Pillars (Post 4–5x per Week)

**1. Event Highlights (2x/week)**
- Carousel or single-image posts featuring top picks from the newsletter
- Post Wednesday (teaser) and Thursday (newsletter day)
- Format: Bold event name, date, venue overlaid on a local photo
- Caption: 2–3 sentences about the event + "More in this week's Signal — link in bio"

**2. Local Scenes (1–2x/week)**
- Original photos: sunsets, beach scenes, restaurant interiors, cocktails, street scenes
- No heavy editing — warm, natural, film-like aesthetic
- Caption: Short, voice-y observation + location tag
- Purpose: Build the brand beyond just events — we're about the South County *vibe*

**3. Polls & Engagement (1x/week)**
- Use Stories or feed polls mirroring the "Signal Noise" section
- "Best lobster roll?" / "Friday night: Westerly or Narragansett?" / "Hot take: agree or disagree?"
- Purpose: Drive engagement, collect content ideas, build community

**4. Behind-the-Scenes / Personal (1x/week, optional)**
- Show the process: "Scouting events for this week's Signal"
- Share a personal South County moment
- Purpose: Humanize the brand, build trust

### Instagram Stories (Daily on Thu–Sun)

**Thursday:** "This week's issue is live" + link sticker → 3–4 story slides highlighting top picks
**Friday:** "This weekend in South County" quick hits — one story per event with location sticker
**Saturday:** Share a photo/video from an event you're at — real-time social proof
**Sunday:** Reshare any stories from businesses/followers who tagged you

### Reels (1–2x/week)

**Content ideas:**
- 15-second "3 things to do this weekend" with quick cuts of local spots
- "POV: A perfect Saturday in South County" — montage format
- "Things tourists don't know about South County" — insider tips
- Event previews — walk-through or countdown format
- "Best [food/drink/view] in South County" — listicle Reels

**Format rules:**
- Always use trending audio when possible
- Add text overlays (many watch without sound)
- Include a CTA in the last frame: "Get the full list — link in bio"
- Tag locations and businesses

## Facebook Strategy

### Facebook Page
- Mirror Instagram content (cross-post)
- Share each newsletter issue as a web link on Thursdays
- Use Facebook Events to promote major featured events

### Facebook Groups (Launch Seeding)

**Groups to join and participate in:**
- South County RI Community
- Narragansett Neighbors
- Westerly/Pawcatuck Area
- South Kingstown Community Board
- What's Happening in South County RI
- Rhode Island Food & Drink
- Things to Do in Rhode Island

**Rules of engagement:**
- Be genuinely helpful — don't just drop links
- Answer questions about events before promoting the newsletter
- Share 2–3 events per week with a note: "I put together a full list of what's happening this week — link to subscribe if you want it every Thursday"
- Build a reputation as "the events person" before being "the newsletter person"
- Never spam. If a group has rules against self-promotion, respect them.

## TikTok Strategy (Phase 2 — Month 3+)

**Handle:** @southcountysignal

**Content approach:** Short-form video versions of newsletter content
- "3 things to do this weekend in South County" (trending sound, text overlays)
- "Rating South County restaurants" (honest, funny, quick)
- "If you live in South County, you need to know about this" (discovery format)
- POV videos from local events and venues

**Why TikTok:** Local content performs surprisingly well on TikTok. The algorithm surfaces content to nearby users, and "things to do" content has high save/share rates.

| Signal Noise | Poll in Stories | Instagram Stories |
