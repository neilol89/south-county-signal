About the Signal

We're not the town calendar.

The friend who always knows what's going on — and isn't afraid to have an opinion about it.

The South County Signal is a free weekly email newsletter curating the best things to do in Southern Rhode Island. Live music, food and drink specials, beach events, nightlife, arts, and family-friendly happenings — all filtered through a local lens with actual personality.

We exist because South County has no single, curated, personality-driven source for weekly events. Information is scattered across Facebook groups, venue Instagram accounts, and word of mouth. We do the work so you don't have to.

Where we cover

Narragansett Wakefield Westerly Watch Hill Charlestown Matunuck Point Judith North Kingstown East Greenwich Wickford + Providence picks

What makes us different

  • 1 Voice We have opinions. The newsletter reads like your most plugged-in friend, not a municipal press release.
  • 2 Curation We don't list everything. We pick the 8-10 best things and tell you why they matter.
  • 3 Consistency Same format, same day, every week. You always know exactly what to expect.
  • 4 Local-first Written by people who live here year-round — not just summer. We know the back roads, the inside jokes, and the real names.
