THE SOUTH COUNTY SIGNAL

Your Weekly Cheat Sheet for Southern RI
Week of March 18, 2026 · Issue #2
The equinox lands Friday, and South County isn't waiting for permission to act like it's spring. There's live music in every direction this week — from a legendary Italian acoustic guitarist in Wakefield to a platinum-selling band up in East Greenwich — and the Knick keeps its comeback rolling with another dance night. If your weekend plan is still blank, fix that now.
THIS WEEK'S MOVE
Better Than Ezra at the Greenwich Odeum
Saturday, March 21 · Evening · Greenwich Odeum, East Greenwich
A platinum-selling, 35-year-running New Orleans rock band in a 400-seat historic theatre? That's not a concert — that's a story you'll be telling at brunch. Better Than Ezra built the '90s alt-rock playbook and they're still sharpening it. The Odeum is one of those rooms where every seat feels like front row. If you've ever hummed 'Good' without knowing who sang it, now you know — and now you can see them do it live, up close.

THE LINEUP
This Week in South County
Live Music
Beppe Gambetta with Victor Main
Saturday, March 21 · 7:00 PM (Doors 6:00 PM) · Pump House Music Works, Wakefield
Internationally acclaimed Italian acoustic guitarist playing an intimate room in Wakefield — the kind of show that makes you wonder how you're seeing a world-class musician for $30. Full bar, all ages, real deal.
Nightlife
Dance Night at The Knick with The Hoolios
Saturday, March 21 · 7:30 PM · Knickerbocker Music Center, Westerly
The Knick's comeback tour continues. Swing music, a 91-year-old dance floor, and The Hoolios doing what they do best. If you missed the reopening — this is your second shot. Don't miss the third.
Arts & Culture
Spring Equinox: Vivaldi & Beethoven
Friday, March 20 · 7:00 PM · UNITED Gallery Event Space, Westerly
Beethoven's Spring Sonata performed on the actual spring equinox — in an intimate gallery space, by two musicians who've played the world's biggest stages. Benefit for the UNITED and Music School. This is the kind of thing South County does quietly and brilliantly.
Nightlife
The Ravers w/ Nena Belén at the Ocean Mist
Friday, March 20 · 9:00 PM · Ocean Mist, Matunuck
Friday night at the Mist with The Ravers and guest Nena Belén. You know the drill — get there before the parking lot fills up and the dance floor takes over.
Fitness & Wellness
Root to Rise: Spring Equinox Sound Bath
Friday, March 20 · 6:00 PM · NURTURE Holistic Wellness, East Greenwich
Ring in the equinox lying down instead of going out. Sound bath, guided relaxation, and a room full of people choosing peace over happy hour. Almost full — book now or wait until summer solstice.
Family Friendly
SoupyFest Bocce | Drop-In
Saturday, March 21 · 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM · UNITED Black Box, Westerly
Drop-in bocce all afternoon at the UNITED. No reservation, no skill required, no excuse not to stop by if you're in Westerly. Part of the ongoing SoupyFest series — which is turning into one of the best things happening down here.
Arts & Culture
Goldfinger — Classic Film at the Odeum
Thursday, March 19 · Evening · Greenwich Odeum, East Greenwich
Bond on the big screen, hosted by the head of the RI TV & Film office. Midweek culture hit for anyone who thinks movies are better when the seats have history.
WHERE TO EAT & DRINK THIS WEEK
Pump House Music Works
Wakefield · 1464 Kingstown Road, right on Route 1
If you haven't been to the Pump House yet, this week's your week. It's a proper listening room tucked into Wakefield — all ages, full bar, and a calendar that punches way above its weight class. The vibe is somewhere between your friend's really nice living room and a folk club in Austin. They book everything from world-class acoustic acts to Sunday jazz to open mics where locals actually show up and play. Beppe Gambetta this Saturday is the move, but honestly — save this one in your phone and check the calendar weekly. You'll be back.
WORTH THE DRIVE Providence
John Scofield Trio
Sunday, March 22 · Evening · Greenwich Odeum, East Greenwich
Grammy-winning jazz guitarist, one of the principal innovators of modern jazz, playing an intimate room 20 minutes up the road. If you care about live music at all, this is the Sunday plan.
